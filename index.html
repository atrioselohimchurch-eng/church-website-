Our Story

About Atrios Elohim Church

Who We Are

A Community Rooted
in Scripture & Love

Atrios Elohim Church is a multigenerational, multicultural community of faith located at 6 Court Street in Flemington, NJ. We are a congregation committed to worship, discipleship, and authentic service — building lives on the unshakeable foundation of God's Word.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of all nations, cultivating a community rooted in Scripture, prayer, and authentic love.

Our Vision

A thriving, multigenerational church where every person encounters the living God, is transformed by the Gospel, and is sent to serve their community and the world.

Our Spiritual Leader

Meet Our Pastor

Apostol Edwin Colop

Senior Pastor — Atrios Elohim Church

Apostol Edwin Colop leads Atrios Elohim Church with a heart for worship, discipleship, and the transformation of lives through the Gospel of Jesus Christ. His ministry is marked by a deep commitment to Scripture, radical hospitality, and a vision for a church where every generation — from every background — encounters the living God.

Under his leadership, Atrios Elohim Church has grown into a vibrant, multicultural community of faith in Flemington, NJ, reaching families and individuals across the region with the love and hope of Jesus Christ.

"The church exists not for itself but for the redemption of the world. Every person who walks through our doors carries a destiny — and it is our honour to help them discover it."

— Apostol Edwin Colop
New Life in Christ

Baptism &
New Beginnings

At Atrios Elohim Church, baptism is one of the most powerful moments in a believer's journey. We have had the joy of seeing many lives transformed and publicly declaring their faith through the waters of baptism.

Ministry Beyond the Walls

Chaplaincy Program

Atrios Elohim Church is proud to commission and support chaplains who serve in their communities, bringing the love and peace of God to those in need. Our chaplains are equipped, certified, and sent to serve.

What We Believe

Our Core Beliefs

01
The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God — our ultimate authority for faith and life.

02
The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

03
Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — available to all who believe.

04
The Holy Spirit

We believe in the ongoing work of the Holy Spirit — empowering believers for life, witness, and service.

05
The Church

We believe the local church is God's primary instrument for advancing His Kingdom in the world.

06
The Return of Christ

We believe in the literal, physical, and glorious return of Jesus Christ to establish His eternal Kingdom.
